Three years of frontier-tier pricing, walked.

The cheapest frontier-tier AI model has fallen ~80× in three years — from GPT-4 at roughly $0.10 / task in March 2023 to DeepSeek V4 Pro at about $0.001 / task in May 2026. The four regime shifts

The floor doesn't slide smoothly. It crashes through plateaus when something specific happens. The four below are the inflection points — each one ended a pricing era.

May 2024 · The OpenAI capitulation
GPT-4o launches at $2.50 / $10 — a 4× cut from GPT-4 Turbo and 12× from the original GPT-4.

OpenAI conceded that frontier-tier wasn't a price-inelastic luxury anymore. The Pro plan stayed, but the API tier had to move to compete with Anthropic Opus and the first usable Gemini Pro. This set the baseline everyone else underbid against for the next year.

January 2025 · The DeepSeek R1 watershed
An open-weight reasoning model from a Chinese hedge-fund spin-out lands within reach of o1's benchmarks at $0.55 / $2.19 — 25× cheaper.

R1 made it impossible for closed-source frontier reasoning to defend a 25× premium with quality alone. The hyperscalers responded by accelerating their own reasoning model price cuts. This is also the moment the "subsidy" narrative became unavoidable — somebody was eating a loss.

November 2025 · The Opus 4.5 cut
Anthropic drops Opus from $15/$75 to $5/$25 — a 67% cut at the highest-margin tier.

Anthropic had held the premium line longest. The November cut was the official acknowledgment that frontier-tier sticker pricing wasn't a moat anymore. From here forward, providers competed on tokenizer efficiency, context window, and integration — not headline rate.

March–April 2026 · The xAI undercut + DeepSeek V4 promo
Grok 4.20 launches at $1.25 / $2.50; DeepSeek V4 Pro launches with a 75% promotional discount taking it to $0.435 / $0.87.

Two new entrants raced the bottom simultaneously. The current frontier floor sits ~55% below SemiAnalysis's mid cost-to-serve estimate. This is the regime the Subsidy Index was built to instrument — somebody is wrong (either the cost-to-serve estimate or the assumption this is sustainable), and the next twelve months should reveal who.
